Welcome to my Web Design Projects!

If you click each image, you will be redirected to the quizzes. The first quiz is a multiple choice quiz testing your knowledge on proper use of commas. The second quiz is a drag and drop quiz testing your knowledge on parts of speech.

Click on the images below to go to each quiz.


These are the changes I made throughout this project:

1. I began by deciding on what I want my quiz to be on and changed the name accordingly. I decided that I would create a quiz on commas. This is why I changed the name to "Ms. Payer's Comma Quiz".

2. I changed the background color to a cool blue color. The exact color is #86B9FF.

3. I put two extra lines before the "Start Over" button.

4. I put a line before the advertisement line at the bottom to separate it from the quiz.

5. I moved the quiz down one line by putting a blank line before the title.

6. I changed the question and answer choices to create the actual comma quiz.

7. I changed the title font and color to be more complimentary with the background color. I chose the font "Century Schoolbook" and chose white for the font to stand out against the blue background.

This was a very interesting and challenging task, but I learned a lot and enjoyed messing with coding!

This has been my favorite project in this course so far. It made a lot more sense to me and was fun to play around with. There was one change I wanted to make but couldn't figure out how. Other than that, I like how my quiz came out and I learned a lot throughout this process!
