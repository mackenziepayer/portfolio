Hello! My name is MacKenzie Payer.

I am currently a 5th Grade teacher in St.Petersburg, Florida. I am a Graduate student at the University of Florida working on a M.Ed in Educational Technology. I hope to pursue a career in instructional design when I graduate from this program in 2023. I graduated from University of South Florida in Spring of 2021 with my Bachelor's in Education. Go Bulls!

Before becoming a 5th grade teacher, I spent years teaching preschool. Now I teach at an alternative education school in St. Petersburg and enjoy this new adventure in education. I began this position teaching 5th Grade ELA until recently when I took on teaching 5th Grade Math and Science as well.

I enjoy teaching in the classroom and building relationships with the students. My favorite part of teaching is seeing the students grow in all aspects of who they are from the beginning of the school year unitl the end. The students are the reason for going into this career. I feel limited in what I can do and become as a school teacher, and that is the reason I am pursuing something more in Educational Technology.
